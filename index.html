Let's Go!

This isn’t your standard campus tour. We’re open, nearby, and ready to
create a personal experience for you. Enjoy all that Wells has to offer.

- Personal tour guide to give you the inside scoop

- Tea Time reception with students, faculty, and admission staff and lunch with an amazing lakeside view

- Guide to local hotspots, including a self-guided walking tour of Aurora

- Complimentary Wells Road Trip Kit

Plus we’ll help you complete your application with a rapid admission and scholarship decision. Once admitted, you’ll
receive a minimum scholarship of $10,000, and be considered for merit-based aid of up to $22,000 a year.
